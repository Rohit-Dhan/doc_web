Our Specialist

Dr. Rohit Rungta

Dr. Rohit Rungta is a Senior & Leading Consultant Nephrologist & Kidney Transplant Physician in Kolkata. His area of practice encompasses not only Kolkata, but Howrah, Hooghly, adjoining areas of Bihar, Jharkhand, Nepal and Bangladesh as well.

He has an experience of more than 15 years and is currently attached to Medica Superspeciality Hospital, Kolkata and AMRI, Mukundapur , Kolkata (for Kidney Transplant only).

He has presented many papers in National and International Journals as well as delivered talks in World Congress of Nephrology at Cape Town, Denmark and Australia for three consecutive years.

He also takes care of teaching programs for post-doctoral trainees in Nephrology, Post Graduate trainees in Medicine and Dialysis Technicians as well.

He is a clinician par excellence, which is clearly evident in the effort he puts behind the holistic management of his patients.
